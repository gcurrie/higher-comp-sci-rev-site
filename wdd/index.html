Web Design & Development

Design and build multi-level websites using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Covers analysis, wireframing, semantic HTML, CSS layout, mouse events and usability testing.

ℹ️
WDD is one of two optional areas — your class will study either WDD or Database Design & Development. WDD contributes 25 marks to the question paper (Section 3) and 15 marks to the assignment (Task 3).
