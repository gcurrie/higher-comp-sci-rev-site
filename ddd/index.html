Database Design & Development

Design, build and query relational databases with three or more linked tables. Covers analysis, ER diagrams, compound keys, data dictionaries and SQL.

ℹ️
DDD is one of two optional areas — your class will study either DDD or Web Design & Development. DDD contributes 25 marks to the question paper (Section 2) and 15 marks to the assignment (Task 2).
